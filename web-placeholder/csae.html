Our Stance Against Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

JusticeLens is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful community. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content that depicts, promotes, or facilitates Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). The safety of children is a paramount concern, and we take our responsibility to protect them very seriously.

Our Commitment and Policies

  • Zero-Tolerance: Any user found posting, sharing, or engaging with CSAE content will be permanently banned from the platform immediately.
  • Proactive Moderation: We utilize a combination of automated AI-driven tools and human review to detect and remove inappropriate content. Our systems are designed to flag material that may violate this policy.
  • Reporting to Authorities: We are legally and morally obligated to report any and all apparent instances of CSAE to law enforcement authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline.

How to Report Violations

Community vigilance is crucial in combating CSAE. If you encounter any content on JusticeLens that you believe violates this policy, please report it immediately.

To report a violation, please email us directly at justicelens.dev@gmail.com with the subject line "URGENT: Report of CSAE Violation". Please include any relevant details, such as screenshots or links to the content in question, to help us take swift action.

All reports are treated with the utmost seriousness and confidentiality.
