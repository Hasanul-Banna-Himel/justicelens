Thank you for your interest in JusticeLens! We're thrilled to invite you to be one of the first to test our community-driven safety and crime reporting app for Bangladesh.

Your feedback is crucial in helping us build a stable, reliable, and impactful platform. Please follow the steps below to get started.

Step 1: Install the App

Our app is available as a closed beta on the Google Play Store. You can download it using the email address this invitation was sent to.

Download from Google Play

Note: You must be logged into the Play Store with this email address to access the beta.

Step 2: What to Test

Once you have the app installed, we'd love for you to focus on testing these key areas:

  • User Onboarding: Can you successfully sign up, sign in, and edit your profile information?
  • Crime Reporting: Try creating a new post. Does the form work as expected? Can you upload an image and select a location and time?
  • Community Feed: Are you able to view posts from other users on the main screen? Does it load correctly?
  • Overall Experience: Pay attention to the app's performance, design, and ease of use. Does it feel intuitive? Do you encounter any crashes, slowdowns, or visual glitches?

Step 3: Report Bugs & Share Feedback

If you find any bugs, crashes, or have suggestions for improvement, please report them on our GitHub repository. This is the best way for our team to track and resolve issues.

When reporting an issue, please be as detailed as possible. Include:

  • A clear, descriptive title.
  • The steps you took to reproduce the problem.
  • What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened.
  • The app version (you can find this in the app's settings).
  • Screenshots or screen recordings, if possible.

Report an Issue on GitHub
